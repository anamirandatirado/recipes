Lasagna


A very delicious-looking lasagna hehe

Description

A lasagna recipe that will make your guests go crazy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sfoglia
  • grinded meat
  • cheese, lots of them!
  • a spice of love

Steps

  1. First, you need to fry your grinded meat with tomato sauce. On an oven tray, spread the pasta
  2. Spread the carnecita on the pasta layer and put another pasta on top, this time, fill it with cheese
  3. TO the oven it goes!
  4. Voila!
